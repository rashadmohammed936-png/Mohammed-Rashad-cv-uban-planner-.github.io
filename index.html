Who I Am

Career Overview

I am a seasoned Urban Planning Engineer and Project Manager with over 13 years of hands-on experience in Saudi Arabia and Egypt. My career spans national and regional urban strategies, rural settlements development, informal settlement classification, and detailed GIS-supported land-use planning.

As a PMP® certified professional and member of the Saudi Council of Engineers, I have led large-scale multi-disciplinary teams and delivered complex projects in collaboration with key government ministries, including the Ministry of Municipalities & Housing and the Ministry of National Guard.

My expertise lies in translating complex spatial data into actionable planning decisions—combining technical mastery with strong project leadership to deliver on time and to specification.
